About

Who we are

Dyson Swarm Technologies is a philosophically driven technology startup. We aim to enter the luxury market with the ultimate goal of making our innovations accessible to the masses.

DST designs innovative, A.I.-powered products that transform everyday household items — and entire industries — into extraordinary, futuristic experiences. Our flagship object, Aegis, uses computer vision to anticipate and position itself beneath incoming drinks. Crafted with unparalleled polish in both hardware and software, it is a statement piece for those looking for a cutting-edge experience.
